Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pair?

To enter pairing mode, hold the power button for 5 seconds until the LED flashes blue and red. Then select "SoundScapes" in your device settings.

Waterproof Rating?

The Sport Run Buds are IPX7 rated (submersible up to 1m). The Studio Pro X are splash-resistant but not fully waterproof.

Battery Life?

Studio Headphones last 40 hours. Earbuds last 8 hours + 24 hours with the case. Speakers vary by model (12-20 hours).

Return Policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied, return it in the original box for a full refund.

Shipping?

We ship worldwide! Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express (1-2 days) is available at checkout.

Warranty?

All products come with a 2-Year Limited Warranty covering manufacturing defects and battery failures.
